Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 April 2026  ·  CoKinnect Ltd

CoKinnect is built for families. We take the privacy of your data — and especially your children's data — seriously. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Who We Are

CoKinnect Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales. We operate the CoKinnect co-parenting communication platform ("the Service"). For data protection purposes, we are the data controller.

Contact: hello@cokinnect.com

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Full name and email address
  • Password (stored as a one-way bcrypt hash — we never see your plaintext password)
  • Account role (Parent A or Parent B) and family pairing code

2.2 Child Information

Special Category Data
  • Child's name and date of birth
  • Allergies and medical notes
  • Doctor and emergency contact details

This is classified as special category data under UK GDPR Article 9. We process it solely for the purpose of facilitating safe, effective co-parenting communication between verified parents.

2.3 Communications & Logs

  • Messages sent between parents within the platform
  • Daily log entries (meals, naps, mood, medication, notes)
  • Schedule patterns and change requests
  • Photos uploaded to the platform (Premium users)

2.4 Subscription & Payment Data

  • Subscription tier and status
  • Stripe customer and subscription IDs
  • We never store card details. All payment processing is handled by Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 certified)

2.5 Technical Data

  • IP address and device type (for security and fraud prevention)
  • App usage patterns (aggregated, anonymised)
  • Error logs and crash reports

3. Why We Collect It (Legal Basis)

  • Contract performance — to provide the co-parenting service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the platform secure, prevent abuse, and improve the service
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable law (e.g. financial records)
  • Explicit consent — where required for special category data relating to children

4. How We Store and Protect Your Data

  • All data is stored on servers located in the UK or European Economic Area
  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12
  • Access to production data is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • We conduct regular security reviews and keep dependencies up to date

5. Children's Data

CoKinnect processes data about children (their name, health information, daily routines) but our platform is used by adults — parents and legal guardians only. We do not knowingly allow children to create accounts or access the platform directly.

Children's data is processed on the basis of explicit consent provided by the registering parent, who confirms they have the legal right to share this information. Both parents in a family unit have access to the shared child profile.

If you believe a child's data has been processed without proper authority, please contact us at hello@cokinnect.com immediately.

6. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your data. We share it only with trusted third-party processors under appropriate data processing agreements:

  • Stripe — payment processing (see Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Cloud infrastructure provider — hosting and storage (UK/EU servers)
  • Error monitoring — anonymised crash reports only

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of a child.

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: personal data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion, except where we are legally required to retain it (e.g. financial records for 6 years)
  • Messages and logs: retained for the life of the family account; both parents must request deletion

8. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — ask us to limit processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, where processing is consent-based

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@cokinnect.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Cookies

CoKinnect is a web app that uses localStorage to store your authentication token on your device. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
