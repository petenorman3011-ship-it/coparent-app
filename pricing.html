Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I switch plans at any time? ⌄
Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the Settings screen inside the app. Upgrades take effect immediately. If you downgrade, you'll keep your current plan's features until the end of your billing period, then switch automatically. There are no penalties or fees for changing plans.
Do both parents need to pay separately? ⌄
No — CoKinnect uses a family portal model. One parent purchases the plan and sends an invite code to the other parent. Both parents then access the full shared portal under the same subscription. You only pay once per family, not per person.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ⌄
Your data is always yours. If you cancel a paid plan, your account moves to the Free tier — you won't lose any existing data, but features above the Free limit will become read-only. You can export all your data at any time (Premium and Family plans) using the in-app export tool. We retain your data for 12 months after cancellation before deletion.
